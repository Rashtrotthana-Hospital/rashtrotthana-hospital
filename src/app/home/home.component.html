About Us

Human Health Care Services

Multispeciality Hospital in Bangalore.

Jayadev Memorial Rashtrotthana Hospital & Research Centre, a 162-bed Integrated Multi-Specialty Tertiary Care Hospital, is recognized as the best multispeciality hospital in Bangalore, offering world-class healthcare facilities. As part of the Rashtrotthana Trust, we provide expert, holistic care in Modern Medicine, Ayurveda, Homeopathy, Yoga and Naturopathy. Our renowned doctors and specialists ensure comprehensive, patient-centered treatment while maintaining affordability and advanced care solutions.
The guiding force behind Rashtrotthana Parishat, Sri M. C Jayadev, envisioned, nurtured and mentored the organization from its inception. As the embodiment of duty and nationalism, his life-long commitment to 'One Life, One Mission' was unparalleled. Rashtrotthana Parishat continues to work towards building a prosperous and healthy society, founded on the strong, enduring principles laid by him. Today, the hospital stands as one of the best multispeciality hospital in Bangalore, contributing to society's well-being through world-class healthcare.
