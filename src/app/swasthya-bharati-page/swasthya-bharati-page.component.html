Lifestyle & Wellness Unit

"समदोषः समाग्निश्च समधातुमलक्रियाः । प्रसन्नात्मेन्द्रियमनाः स्वस्थ इत्यभिधीयते।।

Samadoṣaḥ samāgniśca samadhātu malaḥkriyā |
Prasannātmendriya manaḥ svastha ityabhidhīyate ||

— Sushruta Samhita · Sutra Sthana · 15.38

According to Ayurveda, true health is a state of complete harmony within the body, mind, senses and spirit.

It exists when the three doshas, Vata, Pitta and Kapha, are in balance; digestion, metabolism and assimilation function efficiently; the body's tissues (Dhatus) are well nourished and maintained; waste elimination occurs properly; and the mind, senses and inner self remain calm, content and joyful. Such a state of dynamic balance is known as Swasthya (health).

An individual's health is largely determined by their lifestyle.

Lifestyle encompasses the habits, attitudes, interests, values and behaviours that shape daily living. It includes dietary practices, physical activity, sleep patterns, emotional well-being, social relationships and spiritual pursuits.

Holistic health is achieved by adopting a balanced and disciplined way of life:

नित्यं हिताहारविहारसेवी समीक्ष्यकारी विषयेष्वसक्तः ।
दाता समः सत्यपरः क्षमावान् आप्तोपसेवी च भवत्यरोगः ॥

Nityaṃ hitāhāra-vihāra-sevī samīkṣya-kārī viṣayeṣv asaktaḥ |
dātā samaḥ satya-paraḥ kṣamāvān āptopasevī ca bhavaty arogah ||

This verse highlights that health is not merely a physical state but the result of wise choices, emotional balance, ethical conduct and healthy relationships. Ayurveda thus views a healthy lifestyle as the foundation for disease prevention, well-being and longevity.

A Way of Living

SwasthyaBharati

For a society that shares a common territory, culture, laws, traditions and values to remain truly organised and progressive, it must be healthy. Every individual who is the most important element of society must become healthy. To achieve this, an individual's way of leading life must be healthy.

What is healthy lifestyle? What are the ways to adopt a healthy lifestyle?

To attain physical, mental, intellectual, social and spiritual well-being, our ancestors have laid down the path of a healthy lifestyle starting from food habits to ethical conduct. Communicating this clearly to people in an easily understandable manner through a detailed study of the lifestyle laid down by our ancestors, supported by scientific evidence, is the work plan of Swasthya Bharati.

“Swasthya Bharati” is our hospital’s unique, drugless lifestyle unit that integrates the heritage wisdom of Ayurveda with the strengths of other systems of medicine. Through this integrative approach, it aims to promote holistic health, encourage lifestyle modification and focus on disease prevention, thereby empowering individuals to lead healthier and more balanced lives.

Why Swasthya Bharati

Modern research consistently shows that nearly 70–80% of today's chronic diseases such as diabetes, hypertension, obesity and mental health disorders are closely linked to lifestyle choices rather than genetics alone. Stress, irregular routines, poor sleep and food habits, emotional distress and sedentary behaviour significantly alter hormonal balance, metabolism, and immunity.

Swasthya Bharati, established in 2021, is an initiative dedicated to promote awareness about healthy lifestyle practices among the community. It recognises that true health extends far beyond food habits and physical activity, encompassing emotional wellbeing, thought patterns, social harmony and spiritual balance.

By bridging ancient holistic knowledge with modern scientific insights, Swasthya Bharati aims to cultivate healthier individuals, harmonious families and a more resilient society.
