Meet Our Honorable Founder Sri M. C Jayadev
The visionary force behind Rashtrotthana Parishat,
Sri M. C Jayadev envisioned, nurtured and mentored the organization from its inception. He epitomized the fusion of duty and nationalism, dedicating his life to the principle of "One Life, One Mission" in an unparalleled manner. Rashtrotthana Parishat is steadfastly moving towards its objective of building a prosperous and healthy society by virtue of the durable foundation laid by him. His legacy serves as a guiding light, propelling RP toward the realization of its noble goals.

As I pen these words, a profound sense of pride and gratitude fills me as I reflect on our remarkable journey. Rashtrotthana Hospital stands tall, a testament to our shared vision and mission – A VISION to create a place of healing, where we strive to provide quality, humane and affordable healthcare to all sections of society and A MISSION to provide exceptional healthcare services that go beyond mere treatment where patients are not merely seen but truly heard and cared for.

Our history, marked by unwavering commitment since inception, is a testament to the dedication of our incredible team. From skilled medical professionals to diligent support staff, each member plays a pivotal role in our ongoing success story. However, our journey doesn't solely revolve around the walls of our hospital. It extends to the lives we touch, the families we support and the communities we serve through our community initiatives. It's in the smiles of recovered patients and the hope we instil in the hearts of those facing challenging times.

At the heart of Rashtrotthana Hospital are our CORE VALUES – compassion, excellence, innovation and commitment. These values are not mere words but the guiding principles that shape our actions and define our identity. They drive us to provide not just medical care but genuine warmth and support to every patient who walks through our doors. As we stride into the future, innovation remains at our ethos. Embracing technological advancements, refining our methodologies and continuously learning are integral to our pursuit of excellence. However, amidst this evolution, our commitment to compassionate care will forever remain unwavering. To everyone who's been part of this extraordinary journey, I extend heartfelt gratitude. Here's to a future marked by unwavering commitment, boundless compassion and continued success in serving our community.

M. P. Kumar

President, Rashtrotthana Parishat

About Rashtrotthana Hospital

Jayadev Memorial Rashtrotthana Hospital & Research Centre, a beacon of compassion, excellence and integrity in healthcare. Beyond the confines of traditional medical facilities, Rashtrotthana Hospital is a place where patients are not merely seen but truly heard and cared for. Our commitment to trust and transparency ensures an unwavering dedication to compassionate care.

What sets Rashtrotthana Hospital apart is its integrated approach to healing. Under one roof, we harmonize Modern Medicine, Homeopathy, Yoga and Naturopathy., offering patients a diverse spectrum of services. This unique fusion combines the precision of modern medical practices with the holistic principles of ancient traditions, providing comprehensive care tailored to individual needs.

Integrity is embedded in every service we provide at Rashtrotthana Hospital. From advanced medical treatments to personalized care plans, transparency is paramount. Our hospital stands as a testament to a holistic vision that goes beyond medicine, offering state-of-the-art diagnostics, specialized treatments and a commitment to both traditional and modern healing practices.

At Rashtrotthana Hospital, we are not just a hospital; we are a healing sanctuary where integrated services converge to create a nurturing environment. Our goal is to not only provide medical treatment but to facilitate a comprehensive approach to well-being, embracing the synergy of diverse healing practices under one roof.
