Nutrition & Dietetics
At Rashtrotthana Hospital, our Nutrition & Dietetics Department provides a full range of dietary services that are patient-centred and designed to promote long-term health. With a focus on disease management and preventive care, our dietitians work closely with patients to develop nutrition plans tailored to their specific health conditions, such as diabetes, heart disease and gastrointestinal disorders. Each plan is crafted to enhance recovery, support immune health and improve overall wellness
Core Services:
  • Comprehensive Nutritional Assessments for both inpatients and outpatients, ensuring dietary needs align with medical requirements.
  • Routine Reassessments to adapt nutritional strategies to evolving patient needs.
  • Integrated Carewith healthcare teams, combining medical and nutritional support for a complete approach to health.
  • Personalized Diet Plans designed to address specific health conditions, optimizing nutrition for patients at all stages of care.
  • Diet Counseling and Education to empower patients with sustainable, healthy eating habits that support lifelong wellness.
