Internal Medicine

The Internal Medicine Department at Rashtrotthana Hospital is dedicated to providing comprehensive, patient-focused care for a wide range of adult health concerns. Our specialists are skilled in diagnosing, treating and managing both acute and chronic conditions, ensuring patients receive long-term support for their wellness journey. Common conditions managed by our team include chronic illnesses such as diabetes, hypertension, cardiovascular diseases and respiratory disorders. Our expertise extends to infectious diseases, including respiratory infections and viral illnesses, as well as preventive care measures like screenings and health assessments to identify risk factors early and promote preventive healthcare.

Our hospital offers advanced diagnostic and treatment options, utilizing cutting-edge imaging tools, blood panels and metabolic testing to guarantee accuracy in diagnosis. We prioritize patient safety with 24/7 emergency services, available for immediate response to acute health needs and provide preoperative and postoperative care to ensure a smooth recovery process. Our internal medicine team places great emphasis on empathy and individualised care, leveraging the latest in medical research and technology to deliver optimal outcomes for each patient. For those seeking expert guidance in managing chronic conditions or overall health, scheduling a consultation with our specialists is the first step towards a healthier life.
