When Should You See a General Medicine Doctor?

General medicine doctors, also called internal medicine specialists, treat everyday health issues and guide you if you need further specialty care. Visit us if you have:

  • High fever or temperature not improving in 2-3 days
  • Dry cough, cold or flu-like symptoms
  • Typhoid disease or stomach infections
  • Diabetes or sugar level monitoring
  • Allergies, mild asthma or breathing difficulties
  • Ongoing chronic diseases like hypertension or thyroid issues

If you’re unsure where to begin, start with general medicine - we assess, diagnose and refer you to other specialists if needed.
