Full-Spectrum Anesthesia Services

Our hospital offers every major type of anesthesia, ensuring accurate pain relief and patient safety before, during and after surgery.
General Anesthesia
General anesthesia is used for major surgeries where the patient must remain unconscious. At the Best anesthesiology hospital in Bangalore, our anesthesiologists carefully assess patients before administration and continuously monitor vitals during the procedure to ensure full-body relaxation and pain control.
Regional Anesthesia
This method targets a specific area of the body, such as with spinal or epidural anesthesia. Commonly used in orthopedic, urologic, or obstetric procedures, regional anesthesia allows the patient to remain awake while remaining pain-free.
Monitored Anesthesia Care (MAC)
Ideal for minor procedures, MAC combines sedation with local pain relief. The anesthesiologist closely supervises and adjusts medications throughout the surgery, ensuring patient comfort and rapid recovery. Our expertise in anesthesia for minor surgeries makes us a trusted 24/7 anesthesia support hospital in Bangalore.
