Anesthesiology

The Anesthesiology Department at Rashtrotthana Hospital is dedicated to delivering safe and comfortable experiences for patients undergoing surgical procedures. Our expert team of anesthesiologists provides full-spectrum anaesthesia care, from pre-operative assessments to post-operative recovery. Prioritizing patient safety and well-being, we use advanced monitoring technology and tailor anesthesia techniques to meet each patient’s specific needs, ensuring the best possible outcomes.

We offer various types of anesthesia tailored to individual needs:

General Anesthesia: Induces sleep and ensures pain-free experience during major surgeries, allowing patients to remain unconscious throughout the procedure.

Regional Anesthesia: Includes techniques like spinal and epidural anaesthesia, targeting specific areas for numbing while keeping the patient conscious. Ideal for surgeries where localized pain relief is needed.

Monitored Anesthesia Care: Suitable for minor surgeries, this option provides sedation and localized pain relief as necessary, with the anesthesiologist monitoring and adjusting anaesthesia levels throughout the procedure

Comprehensive Support:Our range of anaesthesia services allows us to cater to a variety of surgical needs, from routine procedures to complex operations, ensuring optimal pain management and patient safety at every stage.

Our anesthesiologists work closely with surgical teams across various specialties, ensuring that each patient’s anaesthesia plan aligns with their overall treatment goals. The department also supports critical care and pain management services, reinforcing our commitment to comprehensive patient care. Additionally, we emphasize patient education, ensuring individuals are informed about their anaesthesia options and any necessary pre- and post-operative precautions.

At Rashtrotthana Hospital, we understand that a successful surgery goes beyond technical precision - it involves a holistic approach to managing pain, minimizing anxiety and optimizing recovery. With a focus on personalized care, our Anesthesiology Department is equipped to handle both planned and emergency procedures, offering comfort, expertise and reassurance to all patients.
