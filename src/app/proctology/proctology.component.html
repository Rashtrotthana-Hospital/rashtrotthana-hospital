What Are Piles?

Piles, or hemorrhoids, are swollen veins in the rectum or anus that can cause discomfort and pain. They are classified into:

  • Internal Hemorrhoids: Found inside the rectum, they are usually painless but may cause bleeding.
  • External Hemorrhoids: These are located under the skin around the anus and can cause pain, itching and swelling.

Recognizing Piles Symptoms

Identifying piles symptoms early can help you get effective treatment. Common signs include:

  • Bleeding during bowel movements.
  • Pain or discomfort while sitting.
  • Swelling or lumps near the anus.
  • Persistent anal itching or irritation.
