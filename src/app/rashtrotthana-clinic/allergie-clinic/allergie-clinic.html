Symptoms
Common Allergy Symptoms include:
  • Sneezing, runny or blocked nose
  • Watery or itchy eyes
  • Skin rashes, itching, or hives
  • Breathing difficulty or cough
  • Reactions after eating or taking medicines
Frequent allergy symptoms shouldn’t be ignored - they may indicate a deeper sensitivity. Identifying the cause through the right allergy test helps your doctor plan effective treatment, ensuring long-term relief and better comfort.
Skin Allergies
Common Skin Allergy Conditions we treat include:
  • Eczema: Dry, itchy, inflamed skin that often flares with environmental triggers
  • Hives (Urticaria): Raised, red, itchy welts appearing suddenly on the skin
  • Contact Dermatitis: Rashes caused by direct contact with soaps, chemicals, or cosmetics
  • Food-related Skin Allergy: Rashes or irritation triggered by certain foods
Our specialists use medications and personalized lifestyle guidance to reduce flare-ups and restore comfort.
Treatment
Allergies occur when your immune system reacts to harmless substances such as dust, pollen, food, or medicines. These triggers, called allergens, can cause discomfort that often repeats and affects daily life if not treated properly.
Every allergy is different - and so is our approach.Our allergy specialists design personalized care plans that target your specific triggers and symptoms.
