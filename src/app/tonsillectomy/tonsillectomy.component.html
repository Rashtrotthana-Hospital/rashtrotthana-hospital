Conditions Treated with Tonsillectomy

Tonsillectomy is advised when enlarged or infected tonsils begin to interfere with breathing, swallowing, sleep, or overall health. Our doctors carefully examine each patient before recommending surgery.

  • Recurrent Tonsillitis (Children & Adults)
  • Enlarged Tonsils
  • Sleep-Related Breathing Problems
  • Chronic Throat Infections
  • Difficulty Breathing or Swallowing
  • Snoring and Sleep Disturbances
