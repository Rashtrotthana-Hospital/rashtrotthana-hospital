About Homeopathy

The word 'Homeopathy' is derived from two Greek words, 'Homeos' means'similar' and'Pathos' means'suffering'. It is based on the natural law of healing "Similia Similibus Curantur" which means "likes are cured by likes". Dr. Samuel Hahnemann (1755 - 1843) gave it a scientific basis in the early 19th century. Homeopathy is an ideal and gentle therapeutic method that offers rapid permanent restoration of health by removing and eradicating the disease to its whole extent in the most harmless way. Homeopathy is a system of medicine in which medicine is chosen based on the individual peculiarities of the patient. The medicine is not selected based only on the disease or diagnosis. Homeopaths consider the totality of symptoms or the total of all symptoms in an individual with the physical and mental constitution of the patient to reach a medicine, that has properties similar to that of the symptom total in the patient or minimum.

How It Work

Homeopathy operates on the principle of treating the individual, not just the disease, using natural remedies to stimulate the body's innate healing ability. It emphasizes the importance of understanding the unique mental, emotional and physical aspects of each person's health. By selecting remedies based on symptom similarity and individual characteristics, homeopathy aims to address the underlying cause of illness rather than just alleviating symptoms. This personalized approach allows for tailored treatment plans that consider the specific needs of each patient, promoting holistic well-being.

Furthermore, homeopathy acknowledges the profound link between mental and physical health, recognizing that emotional stress and psychological factors can manifest as physical symptoms. By addressing these underlying emotional imbalances, homeopathy seeks to restore harmony and balance within the body, promoting overall wellness. Its efficacy extends beyond humans, as it can also be safely and effectively used to treat animals, reflecting its versatility and holistic approach to healing.
