Advanced Urology Care at Rashtrotthana Hospital, Bangalore

The urinary system - including the kidneys, urinary bladder, and urinary organs and genitalia - plays a vital role in removing toxins from the body and maintaining fluid balance. When problems occur, such as kidney stones, urinary tract infections (UTI), or bladder deformities, they can affect daily life and lead to serious complications if untreated.

At Rashtrotthana Hospital, our urology department offers comprehensive care for all urological problems - from routine infections to complex kidney disease. With experienced specialists, modern diagnostic tools and minimally invasive procedures, we provide safe, effective and affordable urological care for patients of all ages.

Comprehensive Urology Services for Your Needs

At Rashtrotthana Hospital, we understand that urological problems require specialized and empathetic care. We offer a full spectrum of services, using modern techniques to ensure the best possible outcomes for our patients. Our team is skilled in various procedures and treatments, from simple conditions like a urine infection to more complex cases.

  • Endoscopic and Laser Treatment:

    We use advanced endoscopic and laser treatments for problems like kidney stones. These methods are minimally invasive, leading to quicker recovery and less discomfort for you.

  • Laparoscopic Surgery for Various Urological Problems:

    Our hospital is equipped for laparoscopic surgery, a modern technique using small incisions. This approach is highly effective for conditions like kidney disease and bladder deformities, resulting in less pain and a faster return to your daily life.

  • Andrology and Male Infertility Services:

    We provide a dedicated service for men's health concerns. Our specialists offer expert diagnosis and treatment for issues related to male infertility and other conditions of the male urinary organs and genitalia.

  • Female Urology:

    We offer specialized care for women's urological issues, including continuous bladder irritation and incontinence. Our team provides discreet and sensitive treatment to help women overcome these common but often difficult problems.

  • Uro-Oncology:

    Our uro-oncology department is dedicated to the treatment of cancers affecting the urinary system. We offer comprehensive and personalized care for cancers of the kidneys, urinary bladder, and other related organs.

  • Stone Disease:

    Dealing with painful kidney stones or back pain? Our team uses the latest technology and techniques to manage and treat stone disease effectively. We aim to relieve your symptoms and prevent future occurrences.

  • Genito-Urinary Reconstruction:

    For patients who need reconstructive surgery to correct issues with their urinary organs and genitalia, our surgeons provide expert care. We are committed to restoring both function and confidence.

  • Paediatric Urology:

    We provide a specialized and compassionate environment for children with urological problems. Our paediatric urology team is trained to handle the unique needs of our youngest patients.
