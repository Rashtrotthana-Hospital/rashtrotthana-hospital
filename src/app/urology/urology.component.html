Recognizing Kidney Disease Symptoms and UTI Warning Signs

Don’t ignore symptoms like:

  • Persistent back pain that worsens over time
  • Blood in urine or unusual urine color
  • Pain or burning sensation during urination
  • Frequent, urgent need to urinate
  • Fever, chills, or fatigue along with urinary issues
These can be signs of kidney disease, kidney stones, urinary bladder infections, or other urological problems requiring prompt care.
