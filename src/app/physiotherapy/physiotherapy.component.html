Why Home Physiotherapy?
Physiotherapy plays a crucial role in restoring movement, reducing pain, and improving overall function. For many patients, travelling to the hospital regularly may be difficult or medically inadvisable. Home physiotherapy ensures that rehabilitation continues safely without interruption.
Home physiotherapy is recommended for patients who:
Have difficulty walking or travelling
Are recovering after surgery or hospital discharge
Require neurological or long-term rehabilitation
Are elderly and at risk of falls
Have chronic pain or complex musculoskeletal conditions
