Embrace the Journey of Motherhood with Ancient Wisdom!
The postpartum phase, known as Sutika Kala in Ayurveda, is a transformative period for both mother and baby. Ayurveda views this time as a sacred opportunity to nurture and rejuvenate the mother, ensuring long-lasting bond between mother and child, promoting overall health and well-being
During Sutika Kala, the mother is advised to follow specific dietary and lifestyle guidelines to aid recovery.Ayurveda also underscores the importance of rest, emotional support, and a calm environment to ensure mental and emotional well-being. These practices not only promote the mother's health but also positively influence the baby's development and growth.
By adhering to these time-tested principles, the postpartum phase can be a harmonious and rejuvenating experience, laying the foundation for a healthy life ahead for both mother and child.
Why Postnatal Care Matters?
Childbirth brings immense joy but also significant physical and emotional changes. The postpartum period is crucial for:
  • Restoring the mother's physical strength.
  • Balancing hormonal fluctuations.
  • Preventing postpartum complications including depression and anxiety
  • Supporting emotional well-being and mental health.
  • Supporting emotional well-being and mental health
  • Enhancing lactation and baby bonding
  • A stronger immune system for mother and baby
  • Faster recovery and healing
