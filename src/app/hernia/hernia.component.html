Types of Hernia We Treat

There are different types of hernias depending on where the weakness occurs in the body. Our doctors carefully evaluate your condition and suggest the most suitable treatment.

  • Inguinal Hernia (Groin Hernia)
  • Umbilical Hernia (Belly Button Hernia)
  • Hiatal Hernia (Stomach Hernia)
  • Femoral Hernia (Upper Thigh Hernia)
  • Incisional Hernia (Post-Surgery Hernia)
  • Epigastric Hernia (Upper Abdomen)
