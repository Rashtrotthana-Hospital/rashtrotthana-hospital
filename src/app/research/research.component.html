Rashtrotthana Research Centre
The Rashtrotthana Research Centre stands as a pioneering institution dedicated to integrative medical research, merging various branches of healthcare including Biomedicine, Ayurveda, Homeopathy, Naturopathy and Yoga. Through comprehensive integrative research, it aims to explore diverse domains of healthcare delivery, validate treatment modalities, practices and lifestyle modifications. Embracing a multidimensional approach, this unique methodology is geared towards enhancing the well-being of humanity and fostering a healthier nation.
Mission and Objectives
The core mission of the Rashtrotthana Research Centre is to undertake and foster research excellence in integrative medicine. This involves both sponsored and in-house research, as well as academic research and investigator-initiated projects. The Centre's activities are reinforced by large-scale organizational projects, population-based epidemiological studies, community-based intervention programs, integrative controlled clinical trials and comprehensive research training programs. These initiatives are designed to support innovative policy thinking aimed at creating a human-centred future for integrative healthcare.
Key Areas
Integrative Clinical Research:
The Centre focuses on integrating biomedicine with AYUSH systems, particularly Ayurveda, wherever appropriate, to enhance patient care and achieve the best treatment outcomes possible.
Research Projects:
Projects range from individual investigator-initiated studies to large organizational initiatives, addressing various health issues through an integrative approach.
Collaboration:
Inter institutional, collaborative research with other National and International Organizations with a MOU institutions/centers of excellence.
Community and Population Health:
Through epidemiological studies and community intervention programs, the Centre aims to understand and address health needs at the grassroots.
Innovations and Technology:
The Centre is committed to developing and translating integrative health technologies into practical applications, benefiting the public health system.
Education and Training:
A key component of the Centre's mission is to provide research training programs that will mould next generation of researchers in integrative medicine.
Future Endeavors
The Rashtrotthana Research Centre is designed to generate innovative policy thinking and solutions that are guided by societal needs. By focusing on integrative health technologies, the Centre aims to bring advancements in diagnosis, treatment methods and their translation into products and processes, ultimately enhancing public health systems.
Through its comprehensive and integrative approach, the Rashtrotthana Research Centre seeks to create a future where Biomedicine and AYUSH systems coexist harmoniously, improving health outcomes and fostering a holistic approach to patient care. The Centre also focuses on periodic review of the progress of Research Projects.
