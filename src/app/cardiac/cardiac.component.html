Advanced Cardiology Services for Every Stage of Heart Care

At Rashtrotthana Hospital, we offer comprehensive heart care services from emergency intervention to complex surgeries. Our experienced cardiologists and heart surgeons combine advanced diagnostics with personalized treatment plans to restore your heart's health and improve your quality of life.

  • Timely Intervention for Heart Attacks and Emergencies:

    When it comes to cardiac arrest or a sudden heart attack, time is everything. Our Interventional Cardiology team is equipped to perform Primary Angioplasty - a critical emergency procedure that restores blood flow and saves heart muscle. We also handle cardiorespiratory arrest with immediate, specialized response, ensuring the best chance at recovery.

  • High-Risk PCI for Complex Cardiac Cases:

    For patients with blocked cardiac arteries, especially those who can't undergo regular surgery, High-Risk PCI (Percutaneous Coronary Intervention) offers a safer alternative. Our experienced cardiologists in RR Nagar, Bangalore manage even the most challenging conditions using cutting-edge techniques.

  • Electrophysiology - Managing Irregular Heartbeats:

    Irregular heartbeats like atrial fibrillation can increase your risk of stroke or heart failure. Through electrophysiology studies, we identify and correct rhythm disturbances, sometimes using devices like an internal cardiac defibrillator to keep your heartbeat steady.

  • Peripheral Vascular Diseases and Circulation Care

    Problems in blood vessels outside the heart can affect your legs, brain or kidneys. We diagnose and treat peripheral vascular diseases to improve your heart circulation and overall cardiovascular health. These conditions often share risk factors with heart disease.

  • Managing Hypertension and Heart Failure

    Uncontrolled hypertension can damage your heart over time. We help you manage high blood pressure effectively to prevent complications. For patients with congestive heart failure or congestive heart disease, we offer advanced monitoring and medication plans to improve quality of life.

  • Full Range of Cardiac Testing Services:

    If you're feeling breathless or notice unusual heart beat rate or chest discomfort, early diagnosis can save lives. We offer ECG, ECHO, TMT, TEE and Stress Echo to assess heart function and detect underlying cardiac conditions before they worsen.

  • Expertise in Cardiac Surgery and Valve Conditions:

    From cardiac valve repairs to full heart surgeries, our cardiac surgeons perform life-saving operations using proven techniques. If you're advised to have a heart operation, you'll be in the hands of experienced specialists who prioritize safety and recovery.
