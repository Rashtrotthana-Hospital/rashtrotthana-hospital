State-of-the-Art Diagnostic & Preventive Care

Advanced Screening for Early Detection
Detecting heart conditions at an early stage is essential for effective management. At our hospital, we use advanced diagnostic tools such as:
  • Echocardiogram (ECHO) - Helps assess heart function and detect abnormalities.
  • Electrocardiogram (ECG) - Identifies irregular heart rhythms and potential cardiac issues.
  • Treadmill Test (TMT) - Evaluates heart response to physical activity, crucial for those with unexplained fatigue or recurring chest tightness.
  • Holter Monitoring - Tracks heartbeat variations to detect abnormalities over an extended period.
A preventive approach, including regular heart checkups, is crucial in maintaining long-term cardiovascular health.

Minimally Invasive & Advanced Heart Treatments

Interventional Procedures for Heart Health
Our team specializes in precision-based treatments that offer effective results with minimal recovery time. Some of the key interventions include:
  • Coronary Angioplasty & Stenting - A vital procedure to restore blood flow in blocked arteries.
  • Pacemaker Implantation - Designed to regulate irregular heartbeats and improve overall cardiac efficiency.
  • Valve Repair & Replacement - Essential for maintaining proper blood circulation and preventing complications.
  • Peripheral Angioplasty - Addresses circulation issues, enhancing vascular health.
These techniques are particularly beneficial for individuals experiencing frequent discomfort, fatigue or dizziness, ensuring that symptoms don’t escalate into serious conditions.
