Obstetrics & Gynaecology

The Department of Obstetrics & Gynaecology (OBG) at Rashtrotthana Hospital is devoted to comprehensive women’s health services, guiding patients through every stage of life, from adolescence to post-menopause. Rooted in the philosophy of Mathruswasthyam Lokaswasthyam- "Mother’s Health is World’s Health"- our department focuses on providing high-quality, compassionate care to women, emphasizing maternal and gynaecological wellness. Our experienced team of obstetricians and gynaecologists are dedicated to delivering affordable and patient-centered care in a supportive environment. At Rashtrotthana Hospital, we prioritise safe and natural birthing practices. Our team emphasizes vaginal deliveries whenever possible, creating a customized birthing plan for each patient. We offer comprehensive maternity packages that cover care from the third month of pregnancy through delivery, ensuring mothers receive the support they need at every stage. Post-delivery, we extend our services to the comfort of your home with our Ayurvedic Abhyanga treatments, specially designed to promote healing and recovery. This unique home care package includes ten sessions of Abhyanga, carefully curated to enhance postnatal wellness and rejuvenation.

Our OBG department is also committed to preventive care, providing regular screenings, pap smears and breast examinations to help women maintain optimal health and detect any concerns early on. In addition to reproductive and maternal services, we offer a specialized yoga package tailored to expectant mothers, covering both prenatal and postnatal needs. These yoga sessions are designed to improve flexibility, relieve stress and support mental and physical well-being throughout pregnancy and recovery. For women experiencing conditions such as endometriosis, fibroids, polycystic ovary syndrome (PCOS) and menopausal symptoms, our team offers individualized treatment plans to address each unique concern. Our multidisciplinary approach ensures that patients have access to specialists from related fields, creating a comprehensive healthcare experience under one roof. By integrating modern medical techniques with compassionate care, the OBG department at Rashtrotthana Hospital remains dedicated to enhancing the quality of life for women at every stage. Our department is committed to making high-quality healthcare accessible for all women, providing not only medical expertise but also personalized care. Whether you’re preparing for childbirth, managing a chronic condition or simply focusing on preventive health, our Obstetrics & Gynaecology Department is here to support your journey to wellness.
