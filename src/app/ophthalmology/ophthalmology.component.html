Pediatric Eye Checkups - Early Vision Screening for Children

Children may not always know how to express vision trouble - and early intervention can shape their learning and development. At Rashtrotthana, we recommend pediatric eye checkups at key milestones:
  • 6 months - 1 year: First baseline screening
  • Around 3 years: Visual coordination and development check
  • Before school entry (4-5 years): Ensure classroom readiness
  • Every 1-2 years after, or earlier if symptoms appear
Signs like itchy eyes and watery eyes, squinting, poor focus, or eye pain should never be ignored. Our dedicated pediatric ophthalmologist doctor ensures your child receives the best start in visual health.

Glaucoma Eye Checkups - Detect It Before It Damages

Glaucoma is a stealthy condition that can gradually damage the optic nerve - often with no early signs. Our glaucoma eye checkups include pressure testing, optic nerve analysis, and visual field screening.
When to screen for glaucoma?
  • Routine screening from age 40
  • Earlier if diabetic, or with a family history of glaucoma
If you're experiencing eye pain, subtle vision loss, or even occasional burning sensation in eyes, schedule an appointment. We provide affordable eye care in RR Nagar Bangalore that prioritizes early detection and safe monitoring.

Cataract Evaluation - Clarity Without Surgery

Cataracts usually develop with age and can gradually affect your daily life. Our team offers non-surgical cataract evaluations using high-end diagnostics to assess cloudiness, lens changes and light sensitivity.
When to start screening?
  • After age 50, or
  • Age 40+ for diabetics or those with past eye trauma
Early signs include glare, blurred vision, frequent eye irritation, and trouble with night vision. While we don’t perform surgery, we guide patients through every step and refer you to trusted surgical experts if needed. We’re known as the best eye hospital in Bangalore for our ethical, transparent approach to cataract care.

Diabetic Retinopathy - Annual Retina Screening for Diabetics

High blood sugar levels can damage delicate blood vessels in the eyes - leading to diabetic retinopathy. That’s why annual retina screenings are a must for all diabetic patients.
Common symptoms include:
  • Sudden vision changes
  • Eye pain or pressure
  • Burning sensation in eyes
  • Frequent red eyes or visual floaters
Our specialists offer accurate retina evaluations and long-term monitoring. Patients seeking an ophthalmologist retina specialist in RR Nagar Bangalore find both experience and compassion here.

Macular Degeneration - Protecting Central Vision for Seniors

Macular degeneration impacts central vision, making it difficult to read, drive, or recognize faces. Our evaluations include retina imaging and personalized care plans that focus on preserving function and slowing progression.
When to screen?
  • From age 50+, especially with family history or smoking
If you're noticing eye pain, focus issues, or constant eye irritation, consult our team. We’re trusted for accurate diagnosis and support in managing age-related retinal decline.
